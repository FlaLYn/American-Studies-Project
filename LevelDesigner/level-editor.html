Asset Browser

Click for one tile. Shift-click one corner, then Shift-click the opposite corner for a block.

Choose a character, then click the canvas to place it. Click a placed character to edit its dialogue.

Click the canvas to place solid collision boxes. These stay editor-only and are not baked into PNG or SVG exports.

Click the canvas to place editor notes. These never appear in the game and export to notes.json only.

Exported PNGs are meant for ../background/levels/level0/background.png. Tile assets are embedded in SVG exports and included in PNG exports. Character spawns, dialogue, hit boxes, and notes stay as editor data and are not baked into the background image.
